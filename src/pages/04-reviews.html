Can I leave a review without a booking?
To maintain the integrity of our botanical community, only verified clients who have completed a service can post to our main gallery. However, you can always mention us on social media!
Do stylists see my feedback?
Yes, we encourage transparent communication. Our stylists value client input as it helps them refine their precision techniques and custom botanical blends.
