Frequent Inquiries

How early should I arrive for my session?
We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes prior to your scheduled time to enjoy our signature botanical tea and complete your digital hair analysis profile.
Do you accept walk-in appointments?
While we primarily operate by appointment to ensure architectural precision, we occasionally have last-minute openings. Please call the studio directly for real-time availability.
Is parking available at the London studio?
There is limited street parking on Floral Street, though we highly recommend using the Covent Garden underground station which is a 3-minute walk away to reduce carbon footprint.
